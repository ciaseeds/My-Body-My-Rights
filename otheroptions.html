Adoption Information:

Many times, women are unprepared, financially unstable, or simply not ready or willing to have a child. If you are a woman who is not ready to be a parent, that is completely alright, and there are multiple other options that an expecting women can consider. Remember to think your choices thoroughly, these decisions will affect you forever so be sure to understand your options and talk to an unbiased professional if that is available in your area. Also remember to talk to family and friends who are reliable, and who will support you through any decision. It’s okay to be scared and confused, but make sure you are making the right decisions for you. Abortion is not murder or an act of treason, it’s not a bad word, or something to be ashamed of, it is completly okay to make this tough desision and choose an abortion, and it is also completly okay for an abotion not to be the right choice for you. Adoption can be a very tough decision but can also be one of the most selfless acts a mother can take. Here are some resources for more information on adoption. You can also search for local adoption agencies around you, but make sure you find a reliable organization.
