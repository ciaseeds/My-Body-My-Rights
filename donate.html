Donate:

Planned Parenthood

Planned Parenthood is a non-partisan non-profit that provides comprehensive educational resources and healthcare, including abortions. They are also focused on preventing STDs and providing screening for different kinds of cancer for both men and women. Planned Parenthood also works locally in Africa and Latin America to improve access to healthcare and education. Donate to Planned Parenthood.

The Guttmacher Institute

The Guttmacher Institute is a research institute that advocates for sexual health education and reproductive rights. The institute collects data and performs studies each year, including some on abortion, which they use to advocate for increased reproductive rights in the United States. Donate to the Guttmacher Institute.

NARAL Pro-Choice America

NARAL Pro-Choice America is a non-profit organization dedicated organizing pro-choice individuals and promoting advocacy opportunities. NARAL has an annual publication on the developments in the U.S. abortion rights debate, and also endorses and promotes pro-choice candidates who are running for office. Donate to NARAL Pro-Choice America.

The Center for Reproductive Rights

The Center for Reproductive Rights is an international organization that aims to solidify laws protecting reproductie rights around the world. Lawsuits let by the Center have helped protect rights and access to healthcare, birthcontrol, abortions, and prenatal care. The CRR believes that reproductive rights are global human rights and advocates internationally to change laws to protect reproductive rights. CRR also presents before the United Nations and publishes comprehensive reports of updates related to reproductive rights and health by region. Donate to the Center for Reproductive Rights.

National Network of Abortion Funds

The National Network of Abortion Funds is an organization that seeks to eliminate barriers to reproductive healthcare. NNAF's funds go towards paying for abortions, or providing other services to make receiving an abortion more affordable and convenient: these include transportation, childcare, lodging and translation services. NNAF has organisations in 38 states, and donations can go towards supporting local abortion funds, or towards a national abortion fund that is distributed as needed to local chapters. Donate to the National Network of Abortion Funds.

EMILY's List

EMILY's List is a non-profit organization that endorses and promotes female democratic, pro-choice candidates running for office. EMILY's List encourages diverse candidates in order to create a more progressive government that enacts change and recognizes minorities. EMILY's List has helped elect 16 governors, 26 senators, and over 100 congresswomen, including Elizabeth Warren, Kamala Harris, Kirsten Gillibrand, Amy Klobuchar and Hillary Clinton. EMILY's List has helped women win elections since 1986 and also provides resources that encourage women to vote. Donate to EMILY's List.
