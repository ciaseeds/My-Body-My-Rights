Access to Abortion Clinics:


Accessibility to abortion clinics varies by state. Only one state, New Hampshire, has legally protected access to abortion clinics (this means no protester can come within a certain distance of the buildings). However, many states have only one abortion clinic, making it difficult for many women to reach their clinic because of large distances. For example, in Missouri in 2014, 94% of women lived in counties without abortion clinics.
